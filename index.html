THE FOUNDER

Built by someone who's lived it.

Rechab founded Valore Group while still in high school in Pittsburgh — building AI tools by day, teaching STEM to students ages 5 to 21 as a Carnegie Mellon STEM Ambassador, and volunteering with ARYSE and JFCS, two organizations supporting refugee and immigrant communities.

His own family's story of displacement shapes how Valore works: value isn't something people have to prove. It's something the world has to recognize.
